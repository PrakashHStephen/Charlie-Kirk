Charlie Kirk

Conservative Commentator & Activist

Charlie Kirk is an American conservative activist and founder of Turning Point USA. He has become known for his commentary on political issues and youth engagement in conservative movements.

He frequently speaks at events and appears on news channels to discuss conservative perspectives and policies.
